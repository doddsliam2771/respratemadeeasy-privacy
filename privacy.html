Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

Resp Rate Made Easy ("the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the app.

Medical Disclaimer

Resp Rate Made Easy is intended for informational and educational purposes only.

The app allows users to manually count breaths and estimate breaths per minute by tapping the screen. The app is not intended for medical use, diagnosis, monitoring, or treatment of any medical condition.

This app does not provide medical advice and should not be used as a substitute for professional medical judgment, diagnosis, or treatment.

If you have concerns about your breathing or health, please consult a qualified healthcare professional.

Information Collection

Resp Rate Made Easy does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

The app works entirely on your device and does not require an internet connection to function.

No personal data, health data, location information, or identifying information is collected by the app.

Health and Usage Data

The app allows users to manually tap to count breaths and calculate breaths per minute. All calculations are performed locally on the device.

The app does not connect to Apple Health, HealthKit, or any other health data services.

Analytics

Resp Rate Made Easy does not use analytics services, tracking technologies, or any third-party tools that collect user data.

Advertising

The app does not display advertisements and does not share any information with advertising networks.

Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate with third-party services that collect or process personal information.

Children’s Privacy

Resp Rate Made Easy does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Data Security

Because the app does not collect or transmit any personal data, there is no personal information stored on external servers.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you may contact the developer through the App Store listing.
